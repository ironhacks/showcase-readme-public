Comparing made easy!

With this tool you'll be able to compare the 59 districts of New York City by criteria such as distance to NYU, number of affordable living units, safety and more!

Click on a criteria button to rank the districts by that criteria. Remember that they'll be ranked by just one of them at a time. Click on that button again to show all the districts by default. If you click on a district's shape you'll enter the district view, where it's stats will be shown in detail. If you use the criteria buttons from here details for that criteria will be drawn over the district using markers. In case of marker oversaturation, a heatmap will be drawn instead. Click the district again or hit the return button to see the whole map again

In the Charts section you'll find detailed information and rankings for each category. Also, you'll be able to download CSV files with information for each criteria
